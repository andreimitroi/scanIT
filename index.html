ScanIT

Aplicatie de car sharing cu masini electrice Twizy (Renault) care pot fi conduse de persoane +16 ani. User-ul isi creeaza un cont unde incarca poza cu documentul de identitate si datele unui card bancar valid. Pentru a inchiria masina, scaneaza QR code-ul masinii si in functie de tariful mentionat, la finalul cursei se retrag automat banii din contul furnizat. Aplicatia noastra ofera o solutie alternativa, rapida si ecologica pentru transportul urban din Bucuresti.

Problem Identification



After analyzing the existing market and already existing car/scooter sharing apps, we arrived at the conclusion that the urban traffic in Bucharest could benefit most from our sharing electric Twizzy Renault car application:

  • first of all from an ecological point of view, given the fact that the cars are electric and also easy to charge and drive
  • second of all, our cars can be driven by any citizen over 16 years old with no driving license
  • our cars are smaller that the usual/existing electric car sharing alternatives and are easier to collect from the streets for charging

Problem Solution



Implementing a car sharing mobile app that will bring on the Romanian Market the posibility of renting an electric car by any person over 16 years old, simply scanning the QR code available on the car at an accesible price.

Customer Discovery



We proved our point by submiting a validation form to various citizens. Our form was answered by 15 persons. Also, we tried to interview few people in order to find out their opinions regarding our app idea/solution.

Focus Groups



The form was answered by mainly young persons over 16 years old that are already using/ have used in the past a traffic sharing application.

Interviews



After completing the form, some participants also showed interest in answering our short live interview to better understand their need of such an application.

Form can be found here and it contains the following questions:

  • Do you want to help the environment and contribute to reducing the global warming process?
  • Do you need an alternative solution to your day to day transportation?
  • Would you prefer an electric car over a normal one? (city wise traffic)
  • On a scale from 1 to 5 how often do you use credit card transactions over cash?
  • What is your eco transportation preference?

Findings

Conclusions



After analyzing our participants answers, we arrived at the conclusion that the Twizy Renault cars could represent a very good, useful and sustainable solution for the current traffic in Bucharest at the moment and with a possible expansion in other major Romanian cities. Our customer discovery has been proved taking into consideration the customers answering, showing in this way the need for scanIT app. Moving forward, we consider that we are ready to move to the Customer Validation stage and start testing the selling of our product.
