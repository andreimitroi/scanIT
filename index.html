ScanIT

Car sharing application offering Renault's electric Twizy cars that can be driven by people from 16+ years old. The user creates an account, uploads pictures of the identity documents and a valid credit card and that's it. To rent a car, simply scan the car's QR code and depending on the mentioned price, at the end of the journey, the money will be automatically withdrawn from the account provided. This app is meant to offer an eco-friendly yet fast transportation solution for Bucharest.

The problem

The problem we identified as being the most annoying in Bucharest is traffic. Day by day the city is getting more and more crowded with cars that are making the transportation from A to B slower and are also hurting the environment.

Our solution

Our solution aims to reduce traffic congestion by encouraging the rental of small and electric cars with zero CO2 emissions, which are easy to drive and park.

The cars can be found and used through a mobile app by simply scanning a QR code.

Customer segment

Our customer segment is represented by the inhabitants of Bucharest. Any person who holds a driving licence for B or B1 class (people from the age of 16) can rent our cars for a trip.

Competition

We have 3 main competitors:

  • Lime and Flow - electric scooters
  • eGo - accesible only through BCR credit card
  • Spark - bigger cars

Our advantage over competition

What we bring to the table are smaller cars that can be driven by people from 16 years old and the posibility to pay with any credit card.

From our survey it seems that the bigger the car, the more affraid people are to rent it. That's where the little and adorable Twizy comes in play.

Key Metrics
  • User engagement - value found by users in our app
  • User retention - how likely is for a user to stick with our app
  • Return on marketing spendings - how efficient a marketing campaign is
  • Customer satisfaction - how satisfied are our customers
The costs
  • Purchase of cars
  • Tracking system for cars
  • Charging of cars

Revenue streams

  • Ride fees - 2RON start + 1.5RON/min
  • In app ads
  • In app memberships - granting no ads and certain discounts

Problem Identification

After analyzing the existing market and already existing car/scooter sharing apps, we arrived at the conclusion that the urban traffic in Bucharest could benefit most from our share based transportation alternative:

  • first of all from an ecological point of view, given the fact that the cars are electric and also easy to charge and drive
  • second of all, our cars can be driven by any citizen over 16 years old with B1 driving license
  • our cars are smaller that the usual/existing electric car sharing alternatives
Problem Solution

Implementing a car sharing mobile app that will bring on the Romanian Market the posibility of renting an electric car by any person over 16 years old, by simply scanning the QR code available on the car at an accesible price.

Market research
Size of the targeted market

Our target market is represented by all the citizens of Bucharest that are over 16 years old & are in the possession of the B1/B driving license category.

Our customers are represented by students/employees that are using car sharing applications and services on a daily basis and are looking for a new and innovative way of transportation for their daily trips to work or school.

scanIT initial target market is represented by citizens of the capital urban area of Romania. The car sharing domain is raising in popularity everyday, as more and more people regardless of age are starting to choose this method of transportation over the public/personal transport.

Aproximate number of players

In this way, our business targets up to 1 million Romanian citizens from Bucharest that own a driving license (category B) plus aprox. 190.000 minor citizens (with ages between 16 years old and 18) that own a driving license category B1.

We plan to further develop our business in other major urban areas of Romania. We also plan to extend to the European market in the distant future if the business evolution riches a higher level.



Competitors
Spark

Comes from Lithuania and has started in Romania in late 2019 with 50 electric cars (Renault and Nissan). It has a fairly good pricing model, the customer paying 1.2-1.4RON/min (depending on the vehicle), but there is a distance limit per day.

eGO by BCR

Car sharing service by BCR which was the first service to give fully electric vehicles to the people and started with a fleet of 20 cars.
Pricing model:

  • with BCR credit card - 35RON for the first 30mins, 20RON/30mins after
  • without BCR credit card - 55RON for the first 30 mins, 1.3RON/min after

Lime

Lime is an american startup which brings 1000 electric scooters in Bucharest. These scooters have the advantage that they don't require a driving license and are easy to manage, but they are not fit for a longer journey and can't be used by more than one person.

Flow

Flow also has aroung 1000 electric scooters in Bucharest and Cluj. Pricing model:

  • 2RON to start + 0.20RON/min
  • 50EURO/month subscription for unlimited rides

Our potential market share

First year (2020)

scanIT is a new application on the car sharing market so our main goal for the first year is to exceed the market share of smaller competition EGO by BCR. In this way our first year will be representing on focusing of users feedback and transactions monitoring. Analyzing the usage of our application and the demand. We aim to have a market share anywhere between 3% - 5% in the first year.

Second year (2021)

In our second year of business we plan to focus on adding new improvements or features to our application. Also, we plan to extend the number of cars in correlation with the client demand (use of our app). We estimate an increase of up to 8% for our market share.

Third year (2022)

In the third year, scanIT app will already have an increased number of users. The raise in popularity potential sponsors and advertising will bring our market share to the next level. We expect reaching a 10-12% for our third year.

Fourth year (2023)

The fourth year will bring even more expansion and development for scanIT. In the year 2023 we plan to bring the application to other major urban ares of Romania (Timisoara, Cluj, Brasov etc). By raising the app usage we expect to reach a market share of 18-20% by the end of 2023.

Fifth year (2024)

For the fifth year, we plan to exceed the current biggest market competitor (Lime) by reaching 30+ %.

Conclusions

After analyzing our participants answers, we arrived at the conclusion that the Twizy Renault cars could represent a very good, useful and sustainable solution for the current traffic in Bucharest at the moment and with a possible expansion in other major Romanian cities. Our customer discovery has been proved taking into consideration the customers answering, showing in this way the need for scanIT app.
