ScanIT

Car sharing application offering Renault's electric Twizy cars that can be driven by people from 16+ years old. The user creates an account, uploads pictures of the identity documents and a valid credit card and that's it. To rent a car, simply scan the car's QR code and depending on the mentioned price, at the end of the journey, the money will be automatically withdrawn from the account provided. This app is meant to offer an eco-friendly yet fast transportation solution for Bucharest.

The problem

The problem we identified as being the most annoying in Bucharest is traffic. Day by day the city is getting more and more crowded with cars that are making the transportation from A to B slower and are also hurting the environment.

Our solution

Our solution aims to reduce traffic congestion by encouraging the rental of small and electric cars with zero CO2 emissions, which are easy to drive and park.

The cars can be found and used through a mobile app by simply scanning a QR code.

Customer segment

Our customer segment is represented by the inhabitants of Bucharest. Any person who holds a driving licence for B or B1 class (people from the age of 16) can rent our cars for a trip.

Competition

We have 3 main competitors:

  • GetPony - few electric cars
  • eGo - accesible only through BCR credit card
  • Spark - bigger cars

Our advantage over competition

What we bring to the table are smaller cars that can be driven by people from 16 years old and the posibility to pay with any credit card.

From our survey it seems that the bigger the car, the more affraid people are to rent it. That's where the little and adorable Twizy comes in play.

Key Metrics
  • User engagement - value found by users in our app

  • Return on marketing spendings - how efficient a marketing campaign is
  • Customer satisfaction - how satisfied are our customers
The costs
  • Purchase of cars
  • Tracking system for cars
  • Charging of cars

Revenue streams

  • Ride fees - 2RON start + 1.5RON/min
  • In app ads
  • In app memberships - granting no ads and certain discounts

Problem Identification

After analyzing the existing market and already existing car/scooter sharing apps, we arrived at the conclusion that the urban traffic in Bucharest could benefit most from our share based transportation alternative:

  • first of all from an ecological point of view, given the fact that the cars are electric and also easy to charge and drive
  • second of all, our cars can be driven by any citizen over 16 years old with B1 driving license
  • our cars are smaller that the usual/existing electric car sharing alternatives
Problem Solution

Implementing a car sharing mobile app that will bring on the Romanian Market the posibility of renting an electric car by any person over 16 years old, by simply scanning the QR code available on the car at an accesible price.

Conclusions

After analyzing our participants answers, we arrived at the conclusion that the Twizy Renault cars could represent a very good, useful and sustainable solution for the current traffic in Bucharest at the moment and with a possible expansion in other major Romanian cities. Our customer discovery has been proved taking into consideration the customers answering, showing in this way the need for scanIT app. Moving forward, we consider that we are ready to move to the Customer Validation stage and start testing the selling of our product.
